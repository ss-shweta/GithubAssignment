1.THE INDIAN EDUACATION SYSTEM


The school system in India has four levels:
lower primary (age 6 to 10), upper primary (11 and 12), high (13 to 15) and higher
secondary (17 and 18). The lower primary school is divided into five “standards”,
upper primary school into two, high school into three and higher secondary into two.
